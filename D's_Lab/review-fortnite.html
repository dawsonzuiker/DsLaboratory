Overview

Fortnite is the true Battle Royale that owns its own category. No other game comes close to it in terms of fun, action, and sheer cultural impact. It is not just a game. It is its own universe.

Gameplay & Feel

Fortnite has more collaborations than any game in the history of gaming. Every match feels different because you are constantly seeing new skins and characters. You could be fighting Peter Griffin, Marge Simpson, or even Kim Kardashian, and somehow it all works. That chaos is part of the magic.

The core gameplay is fast, smooth, and endlessly fun. Playing squads with friends is where the game truly shines. A four player squad can easily lose track of time, turning a quick session into an all night grind without even realizing it.

I have been playing Fortnite since 2018, and it has stayed in my regular rotation for years. With constant updates, new modes, and fresh content, the game never feels stale.

Final Thoughts

With over 3,500 hours played, Fortnite remains one of the most fun multiplayer experiences ever made. It is addicting, endlessly replayable, and unmatched in the Battle Royale space. Fortnite is the best Battle Royale game of all time.
