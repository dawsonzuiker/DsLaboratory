Overview

Mario Kart 8 Deluxe is the true compendium of the Mario Kart franchise. It brings together decades of tracks, mechanics, and ideas into one complete package and stands as the most content rich Mario Kart ever released.

Gameplay & Feel

With the Booster Course Pass included, the game features a total of 96 tracks, the most in Mario Kart history. The DLC adds classic courses from Mario Kart Wii, Double Dash, Mario Kart 64, and more, giving the game a massive sense of variety and longevity.

The customization system adds depth beyond simple character selection. Choosing different karts, wheels, and gliders affects handling, speed, and weight, letting players fine tune their setup while still keeping races accessible and fun. Every race feels a little different depending on how you build your kart.

The tracks themselves are consistently excellent. Courses like Mushroom Gorge, Rainbow Road, and Toad’s Turnpike are visually beautiful and feel great to drive. The game strikes a perfect balance between party chaos and skill based racing, making it fun for casual players and competitive players alike.

I fully completed the game, earning three stars on every cup at every speed, including mirrored mode. Even after doing everything, it is a game I still come back to regularly because it simply never stops being fun.

Final Thoughts

Mario Kart 8 Deluxe is the magnum opus of modern Mario Kart. While it does not rely on nostalgia, it delivers unmatched polish, content, and replayability. It is the definitive Mario Kart experience and one of the best party games ever made.
